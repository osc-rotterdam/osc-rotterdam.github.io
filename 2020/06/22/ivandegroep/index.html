OSCR Member: Ilse van de Groep

2020-06-22 in people

Description

Ilse van de Groep is a PhD Candidate at the SYNC lab (Erasmus University Rotterdam) and the Child and Adolescent Psychiatry Department (Amsterdam UMC, location VUmc). In her PhD project, Ilse examines several mechanisms that underlie distinct developmental trajectories of social and antisocial behavior in emerging adulthood, with a specific focus on the neural correlates of self-concept, vicarious reward learning, social evaluation and aggression regulation.
