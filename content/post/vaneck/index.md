# About me

I am a PhD researcher in health sciences, focusing on consultancy practices, knowledge dynamics, and the politics of expertise in healthcare. My research interests include epistemology, power relations, and how ideas and practices travel across institutional settings. For me, a PhD is not only about developing research ideas, but also about learning what it means to practice research responsibly. I see it as a journey of developing good praxis, which can be vulnerable, honest, and fun. I am curious to learn more about open science and how it can help me make my work both more transparent and more meaningful for others.
