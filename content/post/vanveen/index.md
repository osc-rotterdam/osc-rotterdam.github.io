# About me

Daudi van Veen is an Assistant Professor in Youth & Family Studies at Erasmus University Rotterdam. He studies how people think, feel, and learn about culture-, ethnicity- and race-related topics throughout the lifespan. He is also a co-host of the podcast Joy in Academia.
