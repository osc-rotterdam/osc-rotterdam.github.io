Description

Studying psychology in the midst of a ‘reproducibility crisis’ shaped the way I learned about science and how I still see research practice today.

After working in the private sector in IT startups, I am happy to have returned to academia as a Data Steward at Erasmus University Rotterdam. In this (new) role, I help researchers think about how they manage their research data and what they can do to integrate open practices in their projects.
