Description

I study the effectiveness of the innovative treatment program KINGS (i.e., Kind IN Gezond Systeem, a Dutch acronym for ‘Child in Healthy System’), which is specifically aimed at reducing posttrauma consequences for children with serious behavior problems and their parents who experience multiple problems.

I am interested in open science and aim to promote and adopt an open and transparent scientific workflow by involving stakeholders (e.g., youth, parents, youth care professionals) at every stage of my research process.
