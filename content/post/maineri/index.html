Description

As data manager of the national research infrastructure for the social sciences and economics (ODISSEI), I am actively involved in enhancing the adoption of Open Science and FAIR principles among researchers and data providers. I am also leading the PDI-SSH funded project Building a FAIR Expertise Hub for the social sciences.
