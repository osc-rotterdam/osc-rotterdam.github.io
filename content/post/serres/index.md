# About me

Coline Serres is a Postdoctoral Researcher on Institutions for Collective Action at the Rotterdam School of Management. Her current work evolves around a longitudinal analysis of institutions for collective action, primarily in the Low Countries. Serres holds a Ph.D. in Economics and Management Sciences from Solvay Brussels School of Economics and Management, Universit√© libre de Bruxelles in Belgium. Her research interests evolve around social ventures, institutions for collective action, governance, and commons goods. In particular, Coline has a strong interest for the legal forms dedicated to for-profit social ventures. Her doctoral research focused on the governance of for-profit social enterprises governing common goods.
