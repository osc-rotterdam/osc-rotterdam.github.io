Description

Lisanne van Houtum works as a postdoctoral researcher within Prof. dr. Neeltje van Haren’s lab at the Child and Adolescent Psychiatry/Psychology Unit of Erasmus MC Sophia Children’s hospital. She is involved in both the FAMILY consortium and PANDA-study, to investigate the risk for and resilience against intergenerational transmission of psychopathology in high-risk offspring from a (perinatal) neurodevelopmental perspective. Lisanne co-coordinates an open science course for (bio)medical PhD students and is involved in the bottom-up Open Science Education project.
