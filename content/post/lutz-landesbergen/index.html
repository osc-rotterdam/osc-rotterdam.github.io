Description

I am a PhD candidate at the Department of Psychology, Education and Child Studies at ESSB and Clinical Developmental Psychology, Faculty of Behavioural and Movement Sciences, at VU Amsterdam. I am also a wife and mother of two girls, and proud owner of two lazy cats.

My PhD topic is the role of cognitive control in the development of externalizing psychopathology, part of the Erasmus Initiative Vital Cities and Citizens. I am also a PhD representative for my department, member of the Dutch Society of Developmental Psychology and member of Peer Relation Research group at EUR. I am a strong advocate of making use of already collected data (e.g., use of DANS) and open science: I am in the process of become more and more ‘open science-y’.
